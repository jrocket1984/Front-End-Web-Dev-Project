How to play Puzzles Famous Game! Questions!

The daily puzzle should show up on the left. The puzzle will not tell you whether or not it is a check mate or just a win in material. The square in the bottom left of the puzzle will be black or white. This indicates who's move it is. Once you think you have solved one of the moves then you press the ">" arrow key. If you ever need to go back a move to rethink the position, then click the left "<" arrow. It will stop letting you make moves once the puzzle is solved. Hint: Most moves are some sort of forced checkmate in 1,2,3 moves. A check is when an enemy King is under attack, but he can still either capture the piece that's attacking him, blocking the piece, or running out of the way of the check. If a King is in check and cannont get out of check on his turn, then this is called "Checkmate" and that king would then lose the game. Good Luck!
