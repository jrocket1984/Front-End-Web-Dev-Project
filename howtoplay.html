Welcome to the wonderful game of chess! The idea is to attack the opponents King with your pieces until you "checkmate him". You never actually capture the other person's king in chess. You "check" them, which means you put their king under attack. To win, you must "checkmate" the enemy king, which means that they are in check and also cannot get out of check on their own move either. That's how you win at the game!
